Terms and Conditions

Purchase
An instrument does not become the property of the buyer until the payment is cleared.
If the instrument is returned to me within one week in the condition it was sold, then the purchase price will be refunded,
less a £10 restocking fee (since I will have to examine and clean the instrument again before it can be resold).

Trial before Purchase
If required, an instrument can be reserved until a purchaser has time to test and/or get a teacher or
experienced person to test it at my premises.
In the case of a beginner, I would strongly recommend that the instrument should be tested by someone knowledgeable.
Instruments will not normally be allowed off the premises until paid for.

For customers unable to visit my workshop
I no longer sell and ship instruments by mail or courier, so I'm afraid you will have to travel.

Guarantee Terms
For instruments sold any guarantee given applies only if the instrument is not maltreated in any way, and does not cover accidental damage.
For repairs, the guarantee is limited to the repairs made, and does not apply to the failure of any part not repaired.
In all cases the guarantee only covers the actual failure, and does not cover any consequential loss or damage.
