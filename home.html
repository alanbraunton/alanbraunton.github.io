I am a professional member of NAMIR - the National Association of Musical Instrument Repairers, and can
offer repair, service and overhaul for flutes, clarinets, bassoons, saxophones, oboes and cor anglais.

Please contact  me using email, phone or Facebook if you wish to make an appointment to see one of the instruments
I have for sale, or to arrange for the repair of your own instrument.

If I am not mending instruments I may be playing bassoon/contrabassoon/bass clarinet in
Devizes Town Band or the North Wiltshire Orchestra, or I may be singing in the Bournemouth Symphony Chorus.

I might also be working on our new campervan, Louise the Danbury Active T5.

Note that payments can now be accepted by card if required, although bank transfer is easier and cheaper for me.
