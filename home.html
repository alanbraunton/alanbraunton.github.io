I am a professional member of NAMIR - the National Association of Musical Instrument Repairers, and can
offer repair, service and overhaul for flutes, clarinets, bassoons, saxophones, oboes and cor anglais.

Please contact  me using either email or telephone if you wish to make an appointment to see one of the instruments I have for sale, or to arrange for the repair of your own instrument.

If I am not mending instruments I may be playing bassoon/contrabassoon/bass clarinet in Devizes Town Band, North Wiltshire Orchestra, or I may be singing in the Bournemouth Symphony Chorus.

I might also be working on our new motorhome, Lulu the Autocruise Vista!

Payments can now be accepted by card if required, although bank transfer is easier and cheaper for me.
